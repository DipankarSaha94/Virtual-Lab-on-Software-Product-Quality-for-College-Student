 Software Product Quality is defined as a field of study and practice that describes the desirable attributes of software products. The main approach to measure the product quality is through the quality attributes. The product quality model defined in ISO/IEC 25010 standard comprises of eight quality attributes which further have sub-categories. Each of these attributes/sub-attributes are measured using certain quality metrics. All these attributes are explained briefly in the below tutorials:

PRODUCT QUALITY ATTRIBUTES

  • Reliability
  • Functional Suitability
  • Compatibility
  • Usability
  • Performance efficiency
  • Security
  • Maintainability
  • Portability
